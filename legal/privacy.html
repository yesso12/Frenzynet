FrenzyNet Privacy Notice

Effective date: February 25, 2026

FrenzyNet stores account, subscription, session, and operational telemetry needed to provide service and security. Billing card details are processed by Stripe and are not stored directly in FrenzyNet databases.

Data We Use

  • Account identity and authentication data
  • Subscription and payment status
  • Device/session and abuse prevention events
  • Operational metrics for service quality

Data Protection

Admin actions are audited. Security controls are enforced for fraud prevention and service integrity.
