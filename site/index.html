FAQ

Do all viewers need their own streaming account?

Yes. FlickFuse synchronizes playback and chat; each viewer uses their own streaming account/session.

Can we use browser without extension?

Yes, `/FlickFuse` works directly. The extension just makes setup much easier and faster.
