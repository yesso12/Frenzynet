## Summary (132 chars max)

## Description
FlickFuse Party lets you watch together in sync with your friends across major streaming websites.

What you can do:
- Start or join a party in one click
- Sync play, pause, and seek with your room
- Use a right-side in-page party rail with chat and reactions
- Copy invite links fast from the extension
- Use mic and camera toggles from the rail
- Enable Ad Shield controls for premium workflows

How to start:
1. Install the extension
2. Open your streaming tab
3. Click "Set a party"
4. Share the room invite
